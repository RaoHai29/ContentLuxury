Especially Written Business Plans For The Best Results!

Are you feeling stuck in the process of writing a business plan, and have to submit it in time? We have the right solution to your problem! At Content Luxury, we provide business plans written for all your needs and requirements; such as for procuring loans, or convincing investors and venture capitalists to work with your company.

Whether you are just starting out as a small business, or have a startup or a growing company, you are going to be required to secure capital for it. If you want to expand your business, make it scalable and take it to the next step, we will help you get there by writing your business plans. We will include all the details needed in a good business plan, such as what your business is about, how it generates revenue, a SWOT (Strength, Weaknesses, Opportunity and Threats) analysis and all your financial projections in the coming years. Using all of this, our professional business plan writers will create the most gripping and interesting plan for you, which will help you secure all the funds you need.

Here Are The Types Of Business PlansWe Offer:

Investor Grade Business Plans:

This is the type of plan you will need if you are getting ready to approach investors for raising capital. May it be VC firms or angel investors, we will write an all-encompassing business plan for you, which will include financial projections, market research, cost and benefit analysis, financial statements and investor return projections, etc. It will be a sure way to get investors interested and secure the capital you need for growth.

Bank Compliant Business Plans:

In another scenario, you might be approaching a bank to ask for an SBA (Small Business Administration) loan, and you need a business plan with extensive market research and summary, growth predictions, and financial statements- we have got you covered. We will create a comprehensive business plan for you to present to the bank and get the loan you require.

Immigrant Investor Business Plans:

In the case in which you are looking to secure L1, EB5, or E2 visas for your business, we can write an immigrant investor business plan for you. This will include Matter of HO compliant statements and strategic employment plans which will detail how you can provide more employment opportunities and can positively impact the community.

Franchise Business Plans:

Lastly if you’re thinking of expanding to a new branch location or buying a pre-existing franchise, you have to look no further. Our expertly written business plans include extensive research of the industry and target markets, competitor analysis, and 2-5 years of financial projections. Using our services, you are sure to secure the right deals and make your business grow!
